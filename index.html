About

The prosumer's guide to speciality coffee

I'm Abraham, known online as Conversing Coffee. I'm a UK-based speciality coffee educator and content creator with a mission to close the gap between what professional baristas know and what home enthusiasts actually get told.

My content lives across Instagram (35k) and TikTok (15k), where I break down complex brewing science into digestible, genuinely useful guidance. That means whether you're dialling in your first espresso machine or hunting down the best single-origin beans on the market.

I don't just talk about coffee. I've built deep relationships with the brands shaping the prosumer coffee landscape, giving me honest, hands-on experience with gear at every price point. When I recommend something, it's because I've used it and would actually recommend it.

I'm also actively seeking podcast, television, and radio opportunities, as someone who communicates naturally on camera and has a genuine passion for the craft, I'm ready to bring speciality coffee to wider audiences.

In a time where the term Cost-of-living-crisis gets used a lot, more and more people want to save money by making coffee at home. Not just coffee, really GOOD coffee & I am here to help them do it!
